Edward Edmund Maximilian George Windsor was born on 2 December 1988 at St Mary's Hospital, London, and grew up in Cambridge. 
He is the eldest child of George Windsor, Earl of St Andrews, son and heir apparent of Prince Edward, Duke of Kent. 
Downpatrick's mother, Sylvana Tomaselli, is a historian of Austro-Italian and French extraction. Diana, 
Princess of Wales, was Downpatrick's godmother, but she died before he got to know her well. Lord Downpatrick is 
close to his third cousin Princess Beatrice of York and to his younger sisters, Lady Marina and Lady Amelia Windsor, a fashion model. 
His great-grandmother Princess Marina, Duchess of Kent, born a Princess of Greece and Denmark, 
was the British royal family's fashion icon in the post-war era.
